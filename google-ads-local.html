How we work · 08

A repeatable 5-step process.

The same sequence for every local business. The unglamorous middle (tracking every call before spending more) is where most of the wasted budget gets saved.

Step 011

Listen

A 60-minute call. I want to know your service area, your best customer, your busiest season — and what "a good month" actually looks like.

Step 022

Audit

I review your Google Ads, Business Profile and website. You get a written audit with prioritized fixes — yours to keep either way.

Step 033

Track the calls

Before spending more, we make sure every call, form and booking is tracked back to a keyword. No more guessing what works.

Step 044

Grow carefully

We widen the radius and budget in steady steps, watching cost per booked customer — not just clicks. No wasted spend.

Step 055

Compound

Weekly keyword cleanup, monthly reviews, more 5-star reviews. The boring part where your cost per lead keeps dropping.
